Our Key Products

Polyester (PES)

High tensile strength, good dimensional stability, resistant to moisture, UV, and most chemicals.

Polypropylene (PP)

Lightweight, hydrophobic, chemically inert, low thermal conductivity, and cost-effective.

Polyamide (Nylon)

Very strong, high abrasion resistance, flexible, and moderately moisture-absorbent.

Aramid (Kevlar®, Nomex®)

Exceptional tensile strength-to-weight ratio, flame-resistant, thermally stable, and abrasion-resistant.

Glass Fiber

High tensile strength, non-flammable, dimensionally stable, with good thermal and electrical insulation.
