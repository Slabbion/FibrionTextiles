Our Services

Serving the mining, agriculture, construction, and geotextile industries, Fibrion engineers high-performance technical textiles designed for strength, safety, and efficiency. Our versatile manufacturing capabilities allow us to develop both industry-standard and custom fabrics at scale. From industrial reinforcement to advanced protective materials, we deliver consistent, high-quality results worldwide.

Our Key Products

Polyester (PES)

High tensile strength, good dimensional stability, resistant to moisture, UV, and most chemicals.

Polypropylene (PP)

Lightweight, hydrophobic, chemically inert, low thermal conductivity, and cost-effective.

Polyamide (Nylon)

Very strong, high abrasion resistance, flexible, and moderately moisture-absorbent.

Aramid (Kevlar®, Nomex®)

Exceptional tensile strength-to-weight ratio, flame-resistant, thermally stable, and abrasion-resistant.

Glass Fiber

High tensile strength, non-flammable, dimensionally stable, with good thermal and electrical insulation.
