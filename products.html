Our Products & Services

Our company based in South Africa, specializes in the manufacturing and supply of high-performance technical textiles for the mining, agriculture, construction, and industrial sectors. We engineer durable, reliable fabrics using advanced materials including polyester, polypropylene, fiberglass, aramid, and specialized geotextiles, tailored to meet the demanding requirements of each application. Our extensive manufacturing capabilities and flexible machinery allow us to develop both industry-standard and custom-designed fabrics at scale. In addition to our standard product lines, we provide expert consultation and custom textile solutions for specialized applications, ensuring optimal performance and reliability. As specialists in technical textiles, we focus on delivering engineered fabrics that meet the highest international standards of quality and durability. We proudly serve clients both locally and internationally, including across Africa, bringing our expertise in advanced textile solutions to projects of all sizes.
